NCAA Basketball Game Prediction Program

Python, Pytorch

Developed logistic regression model that predicts the outcome of basketball games at a ~97% accuracy given the final box score and derived advanced stats from NCAA College basketball games.

Constructed algorithm to predict the future box score to train logistic regression model on. Produced stats that were ~30% accurate to the real statline.

Developed two team ranking systems based off scoring and overall efficiency as well as algorithm that significantly reduced model overfitting.

When put together, achieved ~73% accuracy in producing the correct outcome of games only using statistics provided before the game.




Unfortunately, I would like to keep my algorithms private given the success of the project, but I would love to share more about each part of the project works, contributes to the success of the project, and new features that are currently being developed, so feel free to reach out about that.
